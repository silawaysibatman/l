My Autobiography

She is that girl—the woman that she is. Jerlyn Bautista Barbin, born on September 2, 2005, in Sorsogon City, is the youngest in her family and the third among her sisters. Despite being the youngest, Jerlyn often takes on the role of the eldest, always ready to lend a hand, offer advice, and provide comfort. Known affectionately as the "therapist" of the family, her ability to understand and empathize with others is unmatched.

Jerlyn is the family’s ray of sunshine, bringing joy and positivity wherever she goes. Though being the youngest comes with its pressures, she faces them with hard work, determination, and resilience. Raised by parents who made countless sacrifices—especially her father, who works abroad—Jerlyn learned early on the value of hard work and gratitude.

Her sense of responsibility extends beyond her family. Jerlyn is a passionate advocate for responsible pet ownership, driven by her experiences with animal cruelty in her community. She believes every living creature deserves kindness and works to raise awareness about proper pet care. With an open heart and a deep sense of empathy, Jerlyn dreams of a world where compassion is extended to both people and animals.

Her creative passions are another integral part of her identity. Whether she’s reading, painting, or writing, Jerlyn finds solace and self-expression through the arts. She imagines that if everything else fell apart, she’d turn to her paintbrush and create vivid worlds on canvas, or perhaps embrace a writer’s life, embodying the elegance of a real-life Lady Whistledown.

Despite her artistic inclinations, Jerlyn is deeply committed to her studies. As a criminology student, she is driven by a personal mission to fight the injustices she has witnessed. Motivated by her experiences with an imperfect justice system, Jerlyn aims to use her future profession to serve and protect her community. Her dream is not just to earn a degree but to become a law enforcer or public servant who champions fairness, equality, and the betterment of society.

She aspires to lead by example, inspiring others with her strong values and unwavering dedication to justice. Her ambition is firmly rooted in her belief in doing what is right, no matter the circumstances.

Jerlyn’s journey is a testament to her belief in hard work, humility, and the power of values to create a brighter future. Guided by love, driven by a sense of justice, and inspired by the sacrifices of her family, she moves closer to her dream of making a real difference in the world. Through it all, she remains true to the principles that have shaped her life, proving that even the youngest can lead with wisdom and strength.
